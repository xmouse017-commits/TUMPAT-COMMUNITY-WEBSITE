Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

What is the purpose of this website? ›
This website is a centralized platform to connect Tumpat residents and Foreign Tourists about what our place offers from our food and traditional cultures, local news, upcoming events, tourism spots, and community resources.
Is this the official government website? ›
No, this is a community-run initiative designed to support locals and tourists. For official government matters, please contact the Tumpat District Council (MDT).
How can I contact the website administrators? ›
You can reach us via the form on our "Contact" page or email us directly at tumpatcommunity@gmail.com.

Community & Volunteering

How can I become a volunteer? ›
We are always looking for help with beach clean-ups and flood relief efforts. Check the "News" page for call-outs or register your interest on the Contact page.
Can students join the volunteer programs? ›
Absolutely! We encourage students from UiTM and local schools to join our programs to gain experience and contribute to society.

Tourism & Events

How can I list my event on this website? ›
We welcome community submissions! Please send us the event details (date, time, venue, and poster) via the Contact page at least one week in advance.
What is the best time to visit Tumpat? ›
Tumpat is great all year round, but the dry season (February to September) is best for beach activities and river cruises.
Is there public transport to the Sleeping Buddha? ›
Yes, you can take a local bus or taxi from Kota Bharu. We recommend renting a car or using a ride-hailing app for easier access to all the temples.

Safety & Emergency

Where can I get updates on flood status? ›
During the monsoon season (November - January), we post updates on our News page. For real-time alerts, please check the official News website.
Who do I call for emergencies? ›
For general emergencies, dial 999. For local district issues, you can contact the Tumpat District Office directly.
